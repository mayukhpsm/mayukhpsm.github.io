Update rhythm

Refresh policy: This page includes a built-in 72-hour refresh reminder and an age counter so you can see when the list should be updated next.
Scope rule: Entries are kept only when they point to public pages, open registration links, event pages, seminar calendars, or recent replay/watch pages that do not obviously require private institutional login.

Source strategy

These source types are the most reliable places to revisit every 72 hours when you want fresh dissertation webinar links without rebuilding the entire page.

Department defense calendars

Graduate schools, doctoral offices, and department seminar calendars often publish the most direct public links for upcoming defenses, especially in management, medical sciences, engineering, and computing.

Event pages and replay pages

Some institutions publish Zoom registration links for future defenses, while others leave a public page or recorded webinar/watch page accessible after the event has ended.
