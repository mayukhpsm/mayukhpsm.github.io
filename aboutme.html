Profile Summary

I am a full-time Data Consultant in the telecom domain, having spent over 15 years crafting business continuity solutions within intricate digital transformation initiatives. My academic journey includes an M.Engg from Jadavpur University and an MBA from IIT Kharagpur. Currently, I am pursuing an Executive Ph.D. at IIM Indore, with research interests spanning nostalgia marketing, podcast marketing, AI in decision-making, and digital consumer behavior.

I authored the book Ethereum Smart Contract Development published by Packt UK and am an Enterprise Design Thinking Practitioner with certifications as a Professional Scrum Master and Cloud Solutions Architect. I spend my leisure time creating podcast episodes and peer-reviewing research articles for international conferences and journals. My work-in-progress paper on Bricolage and Organizational Nostalgia is currently under review at Organization Studies (FT-50).
